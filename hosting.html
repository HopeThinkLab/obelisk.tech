APPROVED HOSTING

Obelisk presents a unqiue opportunity for those interested in the benefits that come from securing the Sia network through the power of ASICs. We realize though, that not everyone has a suitable space that can handle the electrical and cooling requirements of mining hardware, especially when operating more than one or two units.

DATA CENTERS / COLOCATION

The main alternative to home hosting is to find a data center (also sometimes called a colocation facility or "colo") where your Obelisks can be hosted. It can be quite time consuming to find a good facility that understands the needs of miners, provides "remote hands" services if your miner needs attention, and offers a complete package at a good price.

OBELISK APPROVED HOSTING

We want owning and operating your Obelisks to be an easy, fun and rewarding experience, so we've done the negotiating for you! Below you will find a list of approved hosting packages at companies with locations across the United States and Europe, and we're working on adding more international locations.

One major benefit of selecting an approved hosting package is that your Obelisks will be shipped directly to your chosen provider. They will receive your Obelisks, unpack them, connect them to power & internet, then send you an email with information on how to login remotely to your Obelisks so you can configure them and start mining.
